About Boyd Acres Neighborhood District

Boyd Acres Neighborhood District is dedicated to strengthening our neighborhood by sharing information, encouraging civic participation, and connecting neighbors with opportunities to make our community even better.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a welcoming, informed, and engaged community where residents can stay connected with neighborhood projects, local events, public meetings, and volunteer opportunities.

What We Do

  • Share neighborhood news and announcements
  • Promote community events
  • Provide access to meeting agendas and minutes
  • Encourage volunteerism and neighborhood involvement
  • Help residents stay informed about local planning and development

Get Involved

Everyone within the Boyd Acres boundary is welcome to participate. Attend meetings, volunteer at community events, or simply stay informed by following us on socials and joining our email list! regularly.
